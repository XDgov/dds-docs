Cards

Cards can be used to combine various components of the Data Design Standards.

Small Cards

Small cards are useful tools for highlighting key datapoints of a given dataset. Because of their size, the amount of information that can be presented in a small card is limited. Small cards should appear in groups of no more than three to prevent information fatigue.
